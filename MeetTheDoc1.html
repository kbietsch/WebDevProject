Meet Dr.Allen

Dr. Allen is a native of Baton Rouge, Louisiana. He received his Doctor of Chiropractic from Life University and graduated in 1993. He has been in private family practice for 18 years. He is proficient in many different chiropractic techniques.

 

Dr. Allen completed his Doctorate degree and license with Advance Standing in 2004 of Clinical Counseling. He completed his pre requisites from Louisiana State University.


Dr. Allen has been married for 36 years, has two married children and two grandchildren.
